Airbnb Room Rental Analysis

REAL ESTATE & E-COMMERCE

Project Background

Airbnb is a global online marketplace that connects people who are looking to rent out their homes, apartments or other properties with people who are looking for a place to stay. The rise of the sharing economy has led to a surge in popularity of short-term rental platforms like Airbnb. Airbnb has revolutionized the way people travel, providing a more affordable and authentic alternative to traditional hotels in the United States. As the popularity of these short-term rentals continues to rise, it becomes imperative to assess the implications of this trend on the local housing market, community dynamics and regulatory frameworks.

Problem Statement:

Despite its popularity, Airbnb has faced criticism for its impact on housing affordability and displacement. In some cities, Airbnb has been blamed for driving up rental prices and making it difficult for residents to find affordable housing. As Airbnb properties attract higher-income tourists and short-term visitors, it can elevate property values and drive up rents, making it difficult for existing residents to afford to stay in their communities.

Objectives:

  • Analyze the distribution, pricing and guest preferences across different regions, property types and amenities.
  • The project seeks to identify popular locations, peak seasons within the Airbnb market, ultimately providing actionable insights for hosts, travelers, and stakeholders.
  • Present insights in a clear and engaging way for stakeholders to leverage decision-making.

Contribution:

  • Helps owners optimize their listings, pricing strategies, and target audience in the U.S. market.
  • Contributes to understanding overall market trends, guest preferences, and potential areas for platform improvement.
  • Utilizes Power BI to showcase data effectively and extract valuable insights.
  • Contributes valuable data and analysis to the understanding of the short-term rental market in the United States.

Scope:

  • Data: This project will analyze data from a relevant source, such as Inside Airbnb or publicly available Airbnb listings data. The scope will define the specific timeframe, geographic limitations and types of properties included.
  • Analysis: Specific areas of focus will be determined, such as pricing trends, regional differences, influence of amenities and guest demographics.
  • Power BI Dashboard: The scope will define the layout, visuals, and interactive elements of the dashboard, ensuring it effectively communicates key findings for different audiences.

Executive Summary:

Overview of Findings:

  • The number of properties has grown significantly since 2022, with a total of 31,000 properties added.
  • The majority of people prefer Entire Homes/Apartments, which account for 77.24%, followed by Private Rooms at 21.71%.
  • Cities like New York, Los Angeles, Hawaii, Broward County and Austin have the highest number of available rooms.
  • Due to their tourist appeal, cities such as Rhode Island, Hawaii, Santa Cruz County, Pacific Grove, and San Diego have average room prices that exceed those in other locations.
  • Hotel rooms are particularly favored in Rhode Island and Hawaii, where the average prices are over $500 and close to $300, respectively. Entire Homes/Apartments in Rhode Island and Santa Cruz County also have high average prices, exceeding $400 and approximately $400 respectively. Private Rooms are most common in Pacific Grove and Hawaii with average prices around $400 and close to $300. Additionally, Shared Rooms are popular in only three regions: Rhode Island, Hawaii, and Santa Cruz County.
  • In cities like Columbus, Portland, Newark, and Salem, there is little demand for Entire Homes/Apartments, Hotel Rooms, Private Rooms, or Shared Rooms. The average prices for Entire Homes/Apartments range from $150 to $200, Hotel Rooms range from $80 to $100, Private Rooms are typically under $100, and Shared Rooms range between $30 and $50.

Indeed the current year 2023 is a good time to rent the room since from the year 2021 onwards, the number of rooms had been surged in Cities named New York with 35k listings, Los Angeles with more than 30k rooms availability and Hawaii with 25k.

Solution:

To achieve the project objectives, advanced data visualization techniques are employed using Power BI enabling interactive exploration and visualization of the Airbnb rental data. Additionally, predictive modeling may be utilized to forecast rental prices, demand fluctuations and other relevant metrics. The solutions provided through this project empower stakeholders with actionable insights and strategic guidance for maximizing opportunities within the United States Airbnb market.

Recommendations:

  • The significant increase in property listings in 2023 suggests high demand. Promote the platform actively to attract more hosts and capitalize on this growth.
  • Focus marketing efforts on highlighting the benefits of entire homes and apartments, which seem to be most popular among users.
  • Invest resources in attracting hosts and guests in the top 5 cities (NYC, LA, Hawaii, Broward County, Austin) where there's high demand and existing listings.
  • Analyze why listings are lower in Pacific Grove, Salem, Bozeman, Fort Worth, and Cambridge. Address any barriers hindering host entry in these areas.
  • Promote specific advantages of Pacific Grove, Salem, etc., like unique experiences or affordability, to attract guests seeking alternatives to higher-priced destinations.
  • Investigate reasons behind the popularity of top cities and the challenges faced in low-listing cities.
  • Ensure users can easily find properties based on their desired location, type, price range, and amenities.
